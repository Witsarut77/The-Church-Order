The Church Order

"The pulse is the prayer; the blood is the sermon."

A comprehensive biological-horror expansion for RimWorld 1.6 , focusing on ritualized blood-letting, advanced genetic engineering, and the fanatical pursuit of "The Sanguine Orthodoxy."

🛠️ Development & Structure

The mod is organized into a standard RimWorld structure for easy patching:

  * `/About`: Metadata and Workshop preview.
  * `/Defs`: XML definitions for all new content.
  * `/Textures`: High-quality `.png` assets with full alpha transparency.
